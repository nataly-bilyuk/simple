This is an instruction for making pancakes

First, mix flour, eggs, salt, sugar, spices and a pinch of soda with milk

Secondly, Heat the frying pan and add some oil

Pour two spoonfuls of batter on the hot frying pan

Turn down the heat

Wait until the pancake dries up

Try to turn the pancake over

If it tears, add more eggs to the batter

Fry the other side of the pancake for half the time

Remove the cooked pancake

Repeat the procedure

Continue until you have run out of batter

Enjoy with cream or jam

you can also wrap some stuffing in the pancakes

Try them with cottage cheese, or fried chicken and mushrooms or stewed fruit

Eat cold or heat up first
